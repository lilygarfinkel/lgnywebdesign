Mobile Optimized Design

About 60% of all web traffic comes from mobile devices. Our websites are coded with a mobile first approach, meaning the default styles for each page are coded for mobile devices, then exceptions are added for desktop devices. This decreases the risk of loading lags in your mobile site. In the fast-paced world of online interactions, it is important to keep a users attention by loading content as fast as possible.

Fast Loading Speeds

Because we code our pages from scratch, there are no server side calls required to load page templates or other plugins. This means your website will load entire pages must faster than sites built from imported templates.

Increased Security

Your website will be a compilation of static webfiles hosted on a private server. Due to the lack of external interactions in order to load your site and it's content, there is not much code to hack into.

Original Page Designs

We don't use a site builder like WordPress or Wix; our websites are 100% hand coded. You will get an entirely unique website custom made to fit your business' needs.
