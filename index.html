FAQs About Small Business Websites

Why do I need a website?

A website is a personal showcase of your business. It increases your visibility, credibility, and provides customers a landing point for questions about your services. A good website will create a feeling of professionalism and trust with a client before you even connect.

Clients who are searching for solutions and are unsure where to start will turn to the internet. Businesses with an established online presence are 50% more likely to land a new lead than those that are not.
